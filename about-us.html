Goals

At F1 Hub, the goal is to create an environment where newcomers to the sport can learn all of the ins-and-outs of Formula 1, and where verterans can gather the latest news and updates in the racing world.
This website was created to provide an all-encompassing Formula 1 informational website. We aim to supply fans with current race statistics, constructors leaderboards and team information such as team principles and engineers.

Our values

Accuracy
Commitment to providing accurate and reliable information about Formula 1 events, news, and developments.
Passion
Demonstrating a genuine love for Formula 1 and a desire to share that enthusiasm with others.
Community engagement
Valuing and actively engaging with the community of Formula 1 fans, fostering discussion, and building relationships.
Excellence
Striving for excellence in content quality, presentation, and user experience.
